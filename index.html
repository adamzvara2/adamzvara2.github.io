About JVS Hydrogen

Engineering partner for hydrogen infrastructure

JVS Hydrogen s.r.o. was established in 2023 as a new-energy subsidiary of JVS Consulting s.r.o., an international consulting company with a long track record in the gas and oil industries. Based in Bratislava, we focus on practical hydrogen engineering and implementation support for the European market.

We operate mainly in Germany, the Czech Republic, and Slovakia, working long-term with gas, oil, and operating companies. Our hydrogen activities grew from real project cooperation, including work with Chinese partners on hydrogen production and distribution solutions for freight transport applications.

Materials Expertise & FFS Calculations

Material selection and evaluation, hydrogen embrittlement considerations, stress and vibration analysis, and fitness-for-service assessments in line with API 579-1 / ASME FFS-1.

05
🏗️

Hydrogen Stations Engineering & Small-Scale EPC

Hydrogen-focused EPC for small-scale turnkey projects, engineering at all levels for modular/container HRS, and hydrogen production stations with electrolysis, purification, compression, and high-pressure storage.

06
🔁

Pipeline Conversion & Hydrogen Injection

Engineering for modification of existing high-pressure gas pipeline systems for 100% hydrogen transport, hydrogen injection into natural gas grids, and evaluation of transmission-capacity impacts.
